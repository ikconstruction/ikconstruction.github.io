Quality

IKC’s successful track record in Project management stems from IK's in-house Quality Management Systems. Our Safety & Quality Management Systems include, but is not limited to:

 

Purchasing

 

This system is one of continuous use to IKC, as the company ensures that all raw materials being purchased do conform to contract requirements and specified material criteria.

 

In-Process Inspection & Testing

 

IKC’s responsibility is to maintain the quality of the steel components throughout the shop fabrication process by performing proper inspection & testing of the components based on ASI criteria and applicable codes &

regulations.

 

Final Inspection & Testing

 

Once the component is fabricated to this final phase, it is not exempt to further testing. In order to ensure strict material conformance, a final inspection & testing is done of the steel component ensuring that the steel is fabricated in total accordance with ASI criteria & approved shop drawing configurations.

 

Control of Non-Conforming Material

 

If any of our steel components appear to be in non-conformance with contractual requirements, the component is re-worked or trashed, depending upon the decision of the Shop Foreman. This will ensure that all fabricated components leaving the shop are of the expected degree of conformance.

Certifications

We are certified with following agencies as an MBE, DBE & SBE:

• Port Authority of New York & New Jersey-MBE

• New Jersey Transit -DBE

• School Construction Authority New York-MBE

• School Authority New Jersey- MBE

• State of New Jersey-MBE & SBE

• Empire State -MBE

 

 

Special Process

 

Any special processes, such as welding, being implemented by our shop, is managed under a system that promotes quality & productivity. It is ensured that all welders are qualified & trained, and that all qualifications & training are per system guidelines and properly documented.

 

 

Process Controls

 

Certain processes, such as Production & Installation, require system management, as well. Various parameters, such as shop working environment, level of cleanliness in shop, document & data control at the Field & coordination between Project Manager & Superintendent demand constant oversight and monitoring to aid in efficient management of the Production & Installation processes. IKC understands this necessity, and is achieving great results from its use.

 

Calibration

 

Measuring equipment should always be functioning at maximum capacity & should always be calibrated at the correct time interval to avoid inaccurate readings or measurements. IK Construction Inc. maintains a calibrated schedule for all measuring equipment usable at the fabrication shop & field.

 

Site Installation

 

IKC understands that a project is successful only if there is a constant flow of productivity from the shop to the field. Field personnel are trained to follow system guidelines in ensuring that installations are done to conform to approved shop drawing configurations. IKC’s installations have brought unrelenting customer satisfaction over the years.

Meet our President

Ian Katwaro

Founder & President

IMr. Katwaroo is an experienced and certified welder, designer and fabricator. He specialized in piping and pipe design, metal fabrication and welding during his twenty years in the engineering and construction industry. Having worked for many established international firms throughout Europe and the Caribbean, Mr. Katwaroo developed skills in construction, design and project management. He was also educated in the United States, England, and Germany. He has taught science, mathematics, physics and welding at The University of The West Indies, Trinidad. Originally a native of Trinidad, Mr Katwaroo came to the United States in 1988 and obtained his U.S citizenship. He returned to the U.S. and continuing his professional career.

 

As a "hands on" owner, he constantly involves himself with the daily field operations to ensure that quality control is being met. His sincere dedication and commitment to his profession, clients, business and employees is well proven over the years.
