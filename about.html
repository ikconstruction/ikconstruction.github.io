About Us

Expert Craftsmanship. Professional Service.

IK Construction Corporation has proven to be a leader in fabricating & erecting structural and miscellaneous steel since 1998. IK Construction continues to foster a legacy of integrity and quality workmanship exemplified by the professionalism extended to our clients, prime contractors, subcontractors, detailers, suppliers, and employees.
