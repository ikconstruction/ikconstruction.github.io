Safety & Quality

Our staff has over 20 years of experience in all aspects of shop safety, rigging, hoisting, man lift and forklift operations. IK Construction knows that safety is of upmost importance during field activity. We also understand the value of quality control and quality assurance. Our company quality management system procedures are constantly being updated to reflect new industry equipment technology and the latest ISO & AISC standards.

In-Process Inspection & Testing

IK Construction maintains the highest quality of our steel components throughout the shop fabrication process by performing intermediary inspection and testing of steel components based on ISO & AISC criteria and applicable codes and regulations.

Final Inspection & Testing

Once the steel members or components are fabricated at their final phase, we proceed with a final inspection and testing of all steel members to ensure that the steel is fabricated in total accordance with AISC criteria and approved shop drawing configurations.

Process Controls

IK Construction delivers high quality steel structures to our clients due to a rigorous seamless coordination among our shop fabrication assembly workers, our project managers and our field quality control managers.

Site Installation

IK Construction understands the value of establishing continuity of our quality management system processes from our shop to the job site. Our field personnel are trained to follow quality management system guidelines to help ensure that all steel structures are installed according to the highest quality expectations of our clients.
